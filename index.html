Selected Publications
Publication
 E-3DPSM: A State Machine for Event-based Egocentric3D Human Pose Estimation
Mayur Deshmukh, Hiroyasu Akada, Helge Rhodin, Christian Theobalt, Vladislav Golyanik
CVPR, 2026
Paper: Coming soon / Project: Coming soon

E-3DPSM is an event-driven continuous pose state machine for event-based egocentric 3D human pose estimation. It fuses latent-state updates with direct 3D predictions, runs in real time at 80 Hz, and improves accuracy and temporal stability.

Publication
 GRMM: Real-Time High-Fidelity Gaussian Morphable Head Model with Learned Residuals
Mohit Mendiratta, Mayur Deshmukh, Kartik Teotia, Vladislav Golyanik, Adam Kortylewski, Christian Theobalt
3DV, 2025
paper / project

GRMM is a full-head Gaussian 3D morphable model with residual geometry and appearance for high-frequency detail and better expression control, achieving interactive real-time rendering at 75 FPS.
